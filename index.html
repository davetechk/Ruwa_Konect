Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering via RuwaKonect.

How long does delivery take?
Delivery times vary based on your area's active queue. The smart widget in our order form calculates estimated arrival times in real-time. Typically, it ranges from 15 to 45 minutes once a dispatch is triggered.
What areas do you cover?
We currently cover major off-campus locations around FUTMINNA including Gidan Kwano, Bosso, Tunga, and Chanchaga. We are continuously working to expand our reach.
Is payment required before delivery?
Yes, to ensure a seamless and cashless dispatch process, payment is made securely online during the order phase. Your funds are protected.
Can I order multiple gallons?
Absolutely. You can order up to 20 gallons at once using our quantity stepper. Larger bulk orders will require contacting support.
